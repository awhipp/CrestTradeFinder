Realtime EVE Trade Finder

How to use this tool

  1. Select which trade hub you will be making purchases from.
  2. Find which item you wish to trade
  3. Legend
    • Item - The Item name to Purchase
    • Buy Price - The cost at your current trade hub per item
    • Buy Quantity - How many you can buy (amount available for purchase, or the amount that you can sell at the destination--whichever is lower)
    • Total Cost - How much it will cost you to buy all the quantity listed (Buy Price * Buy Quantity)
    • Sell At - Your destination trade hub for this trade
    • Sell Price - Cost per item that the buy order is asking for at the trade hub
    • Total Profit - (Buy Quantity * Sell Price)
    • Profit Per Item - (Sell Price - Buy Price)
    • Return on Investment = (Revenue - Cost) / Cost
  4. "Sell at" references the hub that is buying at a higher price
