Item
The Item name to Purchase

Buy Price
The cost at your current trade hub per item

Buy Quantity
Whichever is lower: Amount available for purchase at current station, or the amount that you can sell at the destination

Total Cost
How much it will cost you to buy all the quantity listed (Buy Price * Buy Quantity)

Sell At
Your destination trade hub for this trade

Total Profit
(Buy Quantity * Sell Price)

Return on Investment
(Revenue - Cost) / Cost

Sell Price
Cost per item that the buy order is asking for at the trade hub

Profit Per Item
(Sell Price - Buy Price)
